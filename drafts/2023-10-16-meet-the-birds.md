## Meet the birds
My birds perch quietly on the corner of my desk at home. With their wide eyes they observe me as I mull over a tricky problem or prepare for a strategy meeting with my stakeholders. In the software development world there is a concept known as the rubber duck, where talking through a problem to an inanimate object can help to reveal the solution. Despite being neither rubber nor duck, my birds sometimes do a pretty good job of getting me unstuck too.

My ducks were created with love by my very talented father, on his garage lathe in my childhood home. They are based on XXXX and made from XXXX. Each one is unique and can express different emotions with the position of their head.
