## Food hygiene standards in the UK

A static infographic showing how food hygiene standards vary by business type across the UK (with the exception of Scotland, where a different rating system is used). The visual uses waffle charts to display proportions by categories.

![](/images/projects/2023-08-05-farnham-food-hygiene/farnham-food-dash.png)

## The story

I wanted to practise exploring geographical data and discovered the [food ratings data](https://ratings.food.gov.uk) available from the UK [Food Standards Agency](https://www.food.gov.uk). I intend to work on some mapping analysis in due course, but for this first project I decided to explore food hygiene ratings across different business types in the UK. It was my first time building a waffle chart, and I found [this article](https://www.vizwiz.com/2019/09/waffle-chart.html) by [Andy Kreibel](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/andy.kriebel/#!/) very helpful in making this visual.
