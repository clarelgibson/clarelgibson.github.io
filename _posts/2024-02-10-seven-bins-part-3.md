## Introduction
Recently, I had a conversation with my mum about the number of different containers she has to sort her household recycling into. In my house we have just one recycling bin, but Mum has four recycling containers (and when you count general waste, garden waste and food waste, seven bins in total). It got me thinking: if you have more bins to sort your recycling into, do you end up recycling more overall? In [Part 2](https://datatranslator.co.uk/blog/seven-bins-part-2) of this series, I described my progress with the analysis. In this final instalment, I present the results.
